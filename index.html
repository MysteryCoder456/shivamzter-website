ABOUT

A home-schooled boy who’s learning procedural texturing and making Minecraft Texture Packs. It all started back in 2016 when I had just started playing Minecraft and later made a Simple Texture Pack which soon got some attention on Planet Minecraft, But I recently decided to overhaul the said Texture Pack and remake it in the best way possible. Currently working on multiple projects and trying to build a community out of them.

PROJECTS

SIMPLISTA

A Simple Texture Pack made by reducing color noise from albedo minecraft textures and adding PBR support. It supports various stuff like- Subsurface Scattering, Porosity, HCM, Ambient Occlusion and Emission. It also includes custom block models & stuff like CTM.
