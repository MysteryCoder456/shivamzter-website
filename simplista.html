SIMPLISTA

A Simple Texture Pack made by reducing color noise from albedo minecraft textures and adding PBR support. It supports various stuff like- Subsurface Scattering, Porosity, HCM, Ambient Occlusion and Emission. It also includes custom block models & stuff like CTM.

NORMAL MAPS
Normal maps are a special kind of texture that provides data to shader to how to handle and interect the light on the surface. In Minecraft, you can store- Parallax Occlusion Mapping (POM) which adds height to the textures and Ambient Occlusion (AO) which gives the texture extra details in absence of a light source- in the normal map.
SPECULAR MAPS
Specular maps contains smoothness, fresnal (f0), HCM, subsurface scattering, porosity and emissive data which can be used by the shader to give the block it's smoothness, reflectiveness and other unbelievable effects.
