INSTALLING JAVA
  • Go to https://www.java.com and click on then again on the next page.
  • Run the downloaded jre-<version>.exe file.
  • Follow the steps and complete the installation.


INSTALLING OPTIFINE
  • Go to https://optifine.net/downloads.php and choose your desired version (preferably the latest) and choose or (Mirror), and again in the next page.
  • Run the downloaded OptiFine_<version>.jar file.
  • Click on Install, and you're done.
  • If you're having trouble installing, watch this video


RAM ALLOCATION (OPTIONAL)
  • Open the Minecraft launcher and head to Intallations.
  • Hover over the OptiFine installation and click on the 3 dots, and then edit.
  • Click on MORE OPTIONS and then under JVM ARGUMENTS replace -Xmx2G with -Xmx4G.
    • Never allocate more than half your system's ram, and no more than 6gb max.


SHADER INSTALLATION
  • We recommend you to use LabPBR compliant shaders, you can find them here https://wiki.shaderlabs.org/wiki/LabPBR_Supported_Packs.
  • Download your desired shader, the download links will be under the links tab.
  • Launch minecraft with the OptiFine installation.
    • Navigate to  
    1. Options
    2. Video Settings
    3. Shaders
  • Click on to open the shaderpacks folder, then drag and drop the downloaded <shader-name>.zip file into the shaderpacks folder.
  • Go back ingame, and select the shader.


RESOURCEPACK INSTALLATION
  • After you've downloaded our packs.
    • Navigate to  
    1. Options
    2. Resource Pack
  • Click on to open the resourcepacks folder, then drag and drop the downloaded <pack-name-version>.zip file into the resourcepacks folder.
  • Go back ingame, and select the resource pack.
